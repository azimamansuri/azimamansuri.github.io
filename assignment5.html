Assignment 5: Silicone molds and casting

In this assignment, we were tasked to design and fabricate a 2-part silicone mold that we will later use to cast at least 4 identical parts (e.g. in plaster). This involves designing a master part in CAD (using Rhino) first, then designing the mold, then designing a mold to cast the mold in.



Meshes STL (Download) Rhino (Download)

Since I purchased the food safe material, I decided to create a mold for a donut baking sheet.

I used the torus shape to create the inner molds and a box square to create the tray base. I then used a boolean difference to create the mold shape.

I then added tubes to allow for the mold to be poured into the structure and added a sphere to help as support between the two sides. For one side I used the BooleanUnion command and for the other side I used the BooleanDifference command.

This was the final result, which I will be sending over to Cura to set up printing.
